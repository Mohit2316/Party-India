FAQ`s



What's Partiko?

Partiko is for Next Generation, with lot in hand to do in a day. Discover fantastic events, pay in two taps and go. Our Event Planners bring you the best events in your city

How Does It All Work?

We feature events as soon as we get confirmation, usually 3-4 days before the event. Keep an eye out within event details for unique Partiko perks such as: late entries, exclusice events, a free something with your booking, or a mouth-watering discount.

How Do I Book An Event?

Select the event that tickles your fancy from the main events screen, tap "Book Now" at the bottom, select the number of tickets, tap "Confirm Booking" at the bottom (add your card details now if it's your first booking), and "Yes" to confirm the purchase. That's it.

How Do I Pay For The Booking?

Once you've entered your Credit card to Partiko, you can buy any event in two taps. To enter your card details, tap "Settings" in the main menu, then tap "Credit Card", and enter your details. You can also add this information when making your first booking.

Are There Any Hidden Fees?

Nope, we think it sucks to sneak in fees and charges just before you confirm your booking. At Partiko, you pay what you see. Always.

How Do I Get In To The Event Once I've Made The Booking?

You'll find your latest booking in the side menu and all your bookings in "My Tickets". At the bottom of each ticket under "How Do I Get In?" you'll find instructions on how to redeem your tickets and enter the venue. If you have any questions, who you gonna call? Partiko's team is here for you 'round the clock, be it early breakfast or last cocktail: tap the "Call Us" button in "Help".

Do Events Sell Out?

Yes Of course, so book up quick. Once an event is sold out, a waiting list will be available. We'll always try our hardest to snag some more for you, but it's not guaranteed.

How Late Can I Book An Event?

If you see it in the app, it's yours for the taking. We'll keep all tickets open until the last possible moment.

Can I Book An Event For Tomorrow Or Later?

Why, yes you can. We usually have events on 3-4 days before start time, but sometimes there’s more added last minute, and even a select few that go on sale several days early. Keep checking Partiko regularly as the top events sell out quickly...why plan when you have Partiko?

Can I Choose My Seats?

At the moment, all the seats are on a "best available" basis. Unless it's general admission or unreserved seating, we'll always let you know what type of seats you are buying, just not the exact seats. If you buy two or more tickets, we'll make sure you all sit together.

Are My Friends Seeing The Same Events As Me?

Not necessarily. Everyone sees individually handpicked events, so you and your mates may see different sets of events. Use the app to share events with your buddies, or better still, snap up tickets for everyone - they'll love you forever.
